b. 30 m
c. 50 m
d. 60 m

Jawaban

→ Mapel : Math
→ Materi : Keliling
→ Bab : Bangun Datar
————————————————

p - l = 5 m
Maka, p = l + 5 m

K = 2 x ( p + l )
110 = 2 x ( l + 5 + l )
110 = 2 x ( 2l + 5 )
110 = 4l + 10
4l = 110 - 10
4l = 100
lebar = 25 m
panjang = 25 + 5 = 30 m
