Jawaban

= 2(2) + 2 = 6

Jawaban

Rumus SQ = 1/2(Q3-Q1)

Q3 = 3/4. F
= 3/4.70 = 52,5
Data ke-52,5 atau 52/53 = 9.0

Q1 = 1/4. F
= 1/4.70 = 17,5
Data ke-17,5 atau 17/18 = 8.0

SQ = 1/2(9.0-8.0)
=1/2(1.0)
