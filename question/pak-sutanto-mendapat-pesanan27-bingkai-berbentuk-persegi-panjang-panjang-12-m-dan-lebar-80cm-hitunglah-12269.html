Jawaban

Pertama kita mencari besar bunga
4.400,000-4000,000
400,000
selanjutnya
400,000=4000,000×8/100×x/12
400,000=320,000×x/12
400,000=320,000x/12
320,000x=400,000×12
320,000x=4.800,000
x=4.800,000÷320,000
