A. Complete each sentence with a properly formed noun clause. 1. John asked me ________________.
a. where was his wallet
b. where his wallet was

2. I couldn't tell him
________________.
a. where was I
b. where I was

3. I don't know ________________.
a. how many children he has
b. how many children does he have

4. ________________ is not important.
a. How close we are
b. How close are we

5. ________________ was sad.
a. What did she say
b. What she said

6. We are not responsible for ________________.
a. what our children say
b. what do our children say

7. Is it true ________________ about you?
a. what did he say
b. what he said

8. I'm not going to tell you ________________.
a. what should you do
b. what you should do

9. I wonder if ________________ from Germany.
a. is he
b. he is

10. The teacher told us ________________ our finished exams.
a. where we should leave
b. where should we leave

Mohon bantuannya bagaimna cara menyelesaikan soal ini

Jawaban

Answer: 

In reported speech, you have to change the order of part of speech in a sentence. This rules applies in both sentence and question. Here's the example: 

sentence: He said 'I have no time for this trivial things.'
reported: He said that he had no time for that trivial things. 

normal question: She asked 'where is my car?'
reported: She asked where her car was. 

yes/no question: She asked 'is he that dumb?'
reported: She asked if / whether he was that dumb. 

Here's the answer for the exercise above: 
1. b. where his wallet was
2. b. Where I was
3. a. how many children he has.
4. a. How close we are
5. b. what she said
6. a. What our children say
7. b. what he said.
8. b. what you should do
9. b. he is
10. a. where we should leave.
