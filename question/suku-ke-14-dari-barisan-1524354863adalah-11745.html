13 = a + 3(3)
13 = a + 9

Un = a + b(n-1)
Un = 4 + 3(n-1)
Un = 4 + 3n -3

ditanya : L juring =?

Jawab :
L lingkaran = π × r × r
L lingkaran = 3,14 × 10 × 10
L lingkaran = 314 cm2

L juring = 45°/360° × L lingkaran
L juring = 45°/360° × 314
L juring = 39,25 cm2
