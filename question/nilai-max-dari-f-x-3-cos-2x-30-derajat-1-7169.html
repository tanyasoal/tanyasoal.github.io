Hutung hasil dari √50 – √2 + √32 – √98

Jawaban

√50 - √2 + √32 - √98
= √(25 x 2) - √2 + √(16 x 2) - √(49 x 2)
= √25√2 - √2 + √16√2 - √49√2
= 5√2 - √2 + 4√1 - 7√2
= (5 - 1 + 4 - 7) √2
= (1)√2 = √2
