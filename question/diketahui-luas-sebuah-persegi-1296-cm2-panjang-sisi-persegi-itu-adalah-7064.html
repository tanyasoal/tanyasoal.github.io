U4= 56

Eliminasi p I dan p II
a + b = 14
a + 3b = 56
-2b = - 42
b = -42/-2 = 21
subtitusi nilai b ke persamaan I
a + b = 14
a + 21 = 14
a = 14 - 21 = - 7
rasio = U2 : U1 = 14 : -7 = -2
