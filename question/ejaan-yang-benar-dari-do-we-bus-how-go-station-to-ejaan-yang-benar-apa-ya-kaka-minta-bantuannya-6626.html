Complete the sentence using the right form passive voice many different languages...... In india

Jawaban

• PASSIVE VOICE •
keyword : simple present

→ many different languages « are spoken » In india

✐ formula
O + tobe + V3
Many different languages are spoken in India

How come?

Passive voice : Subject + Be + V3
In this case, we use "are" because the subject is plural (you can see by the end of the word language, there is an "s") and we combine it with spoken (because it is said in passive voice, jadi v3 nya speak adalah spoken)

Degree of Comparison
  1. Mention three forms of comparison degree? Positive degree, Comparative degree, and Superlative degree
  2. Explain about positive degree and give 5 examples in sentences?
Positive Degree  => digunakan untuk menyatakan perbandingan yang setara, positive degree harus digunakan bersama correlative conjunction as … as. Positive degree berupa bentuk standar adjective dan adverb tanpa perubahan apapun.  Contoh Positive Degree:  
  • adjective: beautiful, difficult, easy, much, tired    
  • adverb: carefully, fast, gently, much, quickly  
Contoh Kalimat Positive Degree:  
  1. The Upin Ipin is as interesting as Sponge Bob square pants. (Upin Ipin sama menariknya dengan si celana persegi Sponge Bob)
  2. My mum's fried rice is as delicious as the food from a restaurant. (Nasi goreng ibuku sama lezatnya dengan makanan dari restoran)
  3. Ameera is as pretty as a famous model. (Ameera sama cantiknya dengan model terkenal)
  4. The task is not as difficult as you imagine.  (Tugas tersebut tidak sesulit yang kamu bayangkan.)
  5. Ameera's car is as expensive as Ammar's car. (Harga mobil Ameera sama mahalnya dengan mobil Ammar)
PEMBAHASAN:Degree of comparison merupakan bentuk adjective (kata sifat) atau adverb (kata keterangan) yang menyatakan perbandingan.
