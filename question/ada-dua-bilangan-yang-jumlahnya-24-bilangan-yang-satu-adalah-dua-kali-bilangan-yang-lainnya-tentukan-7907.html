Jawaban

Langsung aja y

V = p × l × t
V = 60 × 45 × 50
V = 135.000 cm³

Q = 25 cm³/d

Q = V/t
t = V/Q
t = 135.000/25
t = 5.400 detik
t = 1,5 jam
