Jawaban

Kelas 10 Matematika
Bab Logaritma

⁵log 100 + ⁶log 2 - ⁵log 4 - ⁶log 72
= ⁵log (100/4) + ⁶log (2/72)
= ⁵log 25 + ⁶log (1/36)
= ⁵log 5² + ⁶log 6⁻²
= 2 - 2
= 0
