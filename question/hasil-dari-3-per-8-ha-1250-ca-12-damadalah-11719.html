Jawaban

Dik = AB = 15 cm
r1 = 20 cm
r2 = 12 cm
Dit = PQ ? Jarak antara kedua pusat lingkrn
Peny :
AB ^{2} = PQ^{2} - (r1-r2)^{2}
15^{2} = PQ^{2} - (20-12)^{2}
15^{2} = PQ^{2} - 8^{2}
PQ^{2} - 15^{2} = 8^{2}
PQ^{2} = 8^{2} + 15^{2}
PQ^{2} = 64 + 225
PQ^{2} = 289 = \sqrt{289} = 17 cm
