Jawaban

M + s = 105
m = 105 - s
4m + 2s = 302
4(105-s) + 2s = 302
420 - 4s + 2s = 302
-(4-2)s = 302-420
-2s = -118
s = -118/-2 = 59
m = 105 - 59 = 46

penghasilan = 46x3.000 + 59x1.000 = 138.000 + 59.000 = 197.000
