Jawaban

→ Mapel : Math
→ Materi : Satuan Luas
→ Bab : Satuan Luas
————————————————
1 ha = 100 dam²
1 dam² = 100 m²
1 are = 1 dam²

0,6 ha + 40 dam² + 10 are
= 60 dam² + 40 dam² + 10 dam²
= 110 dam²
= 11.000 m²
