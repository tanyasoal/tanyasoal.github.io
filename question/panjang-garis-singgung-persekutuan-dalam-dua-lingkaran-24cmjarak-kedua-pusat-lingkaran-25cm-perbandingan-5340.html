Jawaban

Kelas 10 Matematika
Bab Trigonometri

PQ/sin R = PR/sin Q
(6√2)/sin 30° = PR/sin 45°
PR = (1/2 √2) . (6 √2) / (1/2)
PR = 6 . 2
PR = 12 cm
