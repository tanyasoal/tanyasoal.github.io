suatu persegi panjang sisi sisinya mempunyai beda 6cm. jika keliling tersebut 52cm maka luas persegi panjang tersebut adalah

Jawaban

Lebar = x
panjang = x + 6

52 = x + x + 6 + x + x + 6
52 = 12 + 4x
4x = 40
x = 10

lebar = 10
panjang = 16
luas = 10×16 = 160

smg membantu, tlg dikoreksi jika salah
>K = 52 cm
>p = (x+6)
>l = x

K = 2p + 2l
52 = (2.(x+6)) + (2.x)
52 = 2x + 12 + 2x
52 = 4x + 12
52 - 12 = 4x
40 = 4x
10 = x (kita temukan lebarnya 10 cm)

berarti :
p = 10+6 = 16 cm
l = 10 cm

L = p × l
= 16 cm × 10 cm
= 160 cm²

jawabannya 160 cm²

f (-3/2) = 9 - 6(-3/2) - 3(-3/2)^2
f (-3/2) = 9 + 9 - 27/4
f (-3/2) = 18 - 27/4
f (-3/2) = 72/4 - 27/4
f (-3/2) = 45/4
