Jawaban

Sudut k = sudut m
2y - 15 = 57 - y
2y + y = 57 + 15
3y = 72
y = 72/3 = 24

K = 2y - 15 = 2.24 - 15 = 33

K + L = 180
33 + L = 180
L = 180 - 33 = 147

L = N = 147
