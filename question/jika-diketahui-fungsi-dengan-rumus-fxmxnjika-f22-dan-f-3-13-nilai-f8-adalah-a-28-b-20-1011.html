Jika diketahui fungsi dengan rumus f(x)=mx+n,jika f(2)=2 dan f(-3)=-13 nilai f(8) adalah A. 28.
B. 20
C. -20
D. -28

Jawaban

➰ Mapel Math
➰ Bab Fungsi

Misal rumus
f(x) = mx + n
f(2) = 2m + n = 2 ........(1)
f(-3) = - 3m + n = - 13 ........(2)

Nilai m
2m + n = 2
- 3m + n = - 13
___________ _
5m = 15
m = 15 / 5
m = 3


Nilai n
2m + n = 2
2(3) + n = 2
6 + n = 2
n = 2 - 6
n = - 4


Rumus fungsi
f(x) = mx + n
f(x) = 3x + ( - 4 )
f(x) = 3x - 4

Nilai f(8)
f(x) = 3x - 4
f(8) = 3(8) - 4
f(8) = 24 - 4
f(8) = 20

Jado, nilai f(8) adalah 20

Opsi => B

Bab Statistika

Siswa putra = a
siswa putri = b

(n(b) - n(a)) . b = (n(s) - n(a)) . s

8 . b = 5 . 80
b = 5 . 80/8
