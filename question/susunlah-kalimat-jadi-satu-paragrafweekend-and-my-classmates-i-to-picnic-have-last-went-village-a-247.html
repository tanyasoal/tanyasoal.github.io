What are the differences between fairy tales and fables

Jawaban

Fairy tale is a story that does not really happen.

The tale contains a brief story about strange and unreasonable things, miracles and supernatural powers that usually tell the gods, kings, princes, and daughters. In general, the tale is unknown to the author and only find out the name of the collector / adapter.

while, Fable is a story that tells the life of animals that behave like humans. The story may not be a true story. Fable is a fictional story, meaning fantasy. Sometimes fables include minority characters of humans.
