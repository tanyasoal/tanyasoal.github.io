Jawaban

Diket =
h = 2cm
s = 20 cm
f = 25cm
Tentukan s', M, h'

1/f = 1/s + 1/s'
1/25 = 1/20 + 1/s'
1/25 - 1/20 = 1/s'
-1/100 = 1/s'
s' = - 100cm

M = |s'/s| = |-100/20| = 5 kali
M = h'/h
5 = h'/2
h' = 10 cm
