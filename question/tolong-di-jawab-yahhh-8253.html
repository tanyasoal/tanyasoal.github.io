Jawaban

→ Mapel : Math
→ Materi : Luas
→ Bab : Perbandingan
————————————————

p : l = 3 : 1
Selisih → 3 - 1 → 2

p = 3/2 x 54 cm
p = 81 cm

l = 1/2 x 54 cm
l = 27 cm

Luas = p x l
Luas = 81 cm x 27 cm
Luas = 2187 cm²
Panjang=3/2×54 cm
=81 cm
Lebar=1/2×54 cm
=27 cm
Luas kebun=p×l
=81cm ×27 cm
=2.187 cm²
