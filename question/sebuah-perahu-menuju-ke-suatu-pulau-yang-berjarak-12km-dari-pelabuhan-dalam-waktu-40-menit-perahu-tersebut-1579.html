Bentuk lain dari 1-cos a/ sin a adalah

Jawaban

Bab : Trigonometri

Ingat !
sin² a + cos² a = 1
sin² a = 1 - cos² a
cos² a = 1 - sin² a

Penyelesaian:
(1 - cos a)/(sin a)
= (1 - cos a)/(sin a) . (sin a)/(sin a)
= [(sin a)(1 - cos a)]/(sin² a)
= [(sin a)(1 - cos a)]/[1 - cos² a]
= [(sin a)(1 - cos a)]/[(1 + cos a)(1 - cos a)]
= (sin a)/(1 + cos a)

Hitunglah (25√5)pangkat -2/3?

Jawaban

(25√5)^(-2/3)
= ((5^2).(5^(1/2)))^(-2/3)
= (5^(2 + 1/2))^(-2/3)
= (5^(5/2))^(-2/3)
= 5^((5/2).(-2/3))
= 5^(-5/3)
= 1/(5^(5/3))
= 1/(5^(1 + 2/3))
= 1/(5^1.5^(2/3))
= 1/(5.25^(1/3))
