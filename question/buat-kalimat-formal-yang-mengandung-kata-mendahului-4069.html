Buat kalimat formal yang mengandung kata mendahului

Jawaban

Answer:

Formal sentences in English are normally used in a formal situation in speaking like academic lectures, press conference or interviews with someone important.
Several things that make the difference from informal language are
- no phrasal verb used.
- no short form a combined word like I've/ I'm
- use formal words

In English, the longer sentences they are, the more polite you are.
Example: I think I'm busy.
Formal one - I'm afraid I am not available.

Here's more formal sentences which including the word 'mendahului' or 'preceding'
- I regret to inform you that our delivery might be preceding your approved deadline. My apology for such inconvenience.
- The preceding documents are seemingly incomplete.

How do you reduce adverbial clauses into adverbial phrases

Jawaban

Frasa partisipial dapat juga dibentuk dari klausa adverbia. Klausa waktu dan alasan yang ditandai dengan kata after, while, when, before, since, because, dan as dapat diringkas menjadi frasa partisipial jika subyek klausa adverbia dan klausa independen sama.

Asalnya: Before ( I left ) home, I promised my parents that I would return.

Diringkas: Before( leaving ) home, I promised my parents that I would return

Diringkas: I promised my parents that I would return before leaving home.

Jika frasa partisipial dari klausa adverbia yang diringkas datang di depan atau di tengah klausa , beri tanda baca dengan koma. Jika frasa partisipial datang setelah klausa independen, jangan gunakan koma

Kapankah saatnya menggunakan kata "Probably" dalam percakapan bahasa inggris?

Jawaban

Kata 'probably' digunakan dalam percakapan bahasa inggris saat dalam situasi formal. Atau juga kata ini digunakan apabila 70 % / 95% untuk terjadi.

David: "Probably, yeah."
