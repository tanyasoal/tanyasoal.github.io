Jawaban

Bruto = 72 kg
Tara = 1 %

=( 72 kg × 5 ) - { ( 72 kg × 1/100 ) × 5} × 3.000
= 360 kg - ( 0,72 × 5 ) × 3.000
=( 360 kg - 3,60 kg ) × rp 3.000
= 356,40 kg × 3.000
= rp 1.069,200
