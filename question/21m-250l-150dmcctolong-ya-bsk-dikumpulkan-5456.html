skala = 1 : 300

Ls = 250×300 = 75000 cm2 =750 m2

maap kalo salah
P = 25 × 300
= 7.500 cm ÷ 100
= 75 m
L = 10 × 300
= 3.000 cm ÷ 100
= 30 m

Lpp = p × l
= 75 × 30
= 2.250
