Jawaban

Volume Limas

= 1/3 × Luas alas × Tinggi

= 88 cm3

Tinggi

= 88 cm3 : 1/3 : 24 cm2

= 88 cm3 × 3 : 24 cm2

= 264 cm3 : 24 cm2

= 11 cm
V = 1/3 x luas alas x t 88 = 1/3 x 24 x t 88 = 8 x t t. = 88 / 8 = 11 cm
