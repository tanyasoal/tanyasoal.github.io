B.63/8
C.63/64
D.31
E.63

Jawaban

R^6-2 = 1/16/1
r⁴ = ½⁴
r = ½
U2 = Ar = 1
a x ½ = 1
a = 1/½ = 2

S6 = 2 x (1 - ½^6)/(1-½)
= 2 x (64-1)/64 x 2/1
= 4/64 x 63
= 63/16
