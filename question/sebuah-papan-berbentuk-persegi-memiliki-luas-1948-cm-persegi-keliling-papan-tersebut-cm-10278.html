Pajang 12cm keliling 76 cm berapa kah hasil pajang sisi persegi panjang

Jawaban

Keliling persegi panjang = 2xpanjang + 2xlebar
                           76        = 2x12 + 2xlebar
                           76        = 24 + 2xlebar
                           76 - 24 = 2xlebar
                           52        = 2xlebar
                           52/2     = lebar
                           26        = lebar
