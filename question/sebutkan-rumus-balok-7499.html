Materi : Logaritma
Kelas : 9

3log 5 = y


= 4log 25
= 3log 25 / 3log 4
= 3log (5*5) / 3log (2*2)
= 2•3log 5 / 2•3log 2
= 2(y) /2(x)
= 2y/2x
