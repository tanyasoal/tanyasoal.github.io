60/x = 80/12

x = 9

720 =80×

x=9

3 . (cosec 45°)² + (cos 45°)² - 2 . (tan 30°)²
= 3 . (√2)² + (√2)² - 2 . (1/√3)²
= 3 . 2 + 2 - 2 . 1/3
= 6 + 2 - 2/3
= 8 - 2/3
