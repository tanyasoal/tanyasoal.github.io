Misalah akar-akarnya a dan b
maka a^3 b^2 + b^3 a^2 = ....?

a^3 b^2 + b^3 a^2
= (a^2 b^2) (a + b)
= (ab)^2 (a+b)
= (c/a)^2 (-b/a)
= ( 1/(-2) )^2 · (-1/(-2))
= 1/4 · 1/2
= 1/8
