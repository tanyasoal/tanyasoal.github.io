V = 150 liter =150 dm^3
p = 1 m = 10 dm
l = 50 cm = 5 dm

v = p x l x t
150 = 10 x 5 x t
150 = 50 x t
t = 150 : 50
t = 3 dm = 30 cm = 0,3 m
