2. There are five characters:
a) Dorothy, she is brave and honest
b) Lion, he is coward
c) Toto, he is brave
d) Scarecrow, he is hopeful
e) Tinman
3. Yes, there was a problem in the text. The Lion is too coward, although he was the King of the Beasts.
4. Yes, finally they invited the Lion to go to Emerald City to meet Oz and ask for courage.
5. Direct speech:
a) "Don't you dare bite Toto!" she shouted.
b) "You ought to be ashamed of yourself, a big beast like you, to bite a poor little dog!"
c) "I didn't bite him," said the Lion
d) "No, but you tried to," Dorothy answered. "You are nothing but a coward!"
e) Then he said, "Maybe Oz could give you courage,"
f) "If only I had courage, then I could truly be the King of the Beasts." said the Lion.

He told Dorothy and her friends that was although he was the King of the Beasts, he was actually a coward.

Mention the signal word about cause and effect ?

Jawaban

1.Because I was late, I lost my place on the queue. (Why/cause before what/effect)

I lost my place on the queue because I was late. (what/effect before why/cause)

2.The medical trials were a disaster; as a result the company discontinued the medication. (why/cause before what/effect)

As a result of the disastrous medical trials, the company discontinued the medication. (why/cause before what/effect)
