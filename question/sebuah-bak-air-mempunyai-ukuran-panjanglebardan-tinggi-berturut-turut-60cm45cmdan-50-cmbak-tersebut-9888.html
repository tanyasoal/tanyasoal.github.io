Jawaban

Volume = p x l x t 
160 l = 160000 cm³
160 000cm³ = 80cm x 50cm x t
160 000cm³ = 4000cm² x t 
160 000cm³/4000cm² = t 
40 cm = t 

maaf kalau salah :)

- panjang = 80 cm (8 dm)
- lebar = 50 cm (5dm)

Jawab :

v. = p × l × t

160 = 8 × 5 × t

160 = 40 × t

t. = 160 : 40

t. = 4



Percobaan :

v = 8 × 5 × 4
v = 40 × 4
v = 160 dm³ (liter)
