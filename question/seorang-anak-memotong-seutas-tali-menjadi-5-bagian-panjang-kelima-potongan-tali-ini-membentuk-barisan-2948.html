Jawaban

subsitusi a = 2 x b
ke persamaan a + b = 15
sehingga,
a + b = 15
(2 x b) + b = 15
2b + b = 15
3b = 15
b = 15/3
b = 5

a + b = 15
a + 5 = 15
maka a = 15 - 5 = 10
jadi a = 10
