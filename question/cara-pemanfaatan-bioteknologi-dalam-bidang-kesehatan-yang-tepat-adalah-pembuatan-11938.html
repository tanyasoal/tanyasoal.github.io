P : l : t = 5 : 3 : 2
p X l = 135 cm2

5x X 3x = 135
x2 = 135/15
x2 = 9
x = 3

p = 5x = 5 X 3 = 15 cm
l = 3x = 3 X 3 = 9 cm
t = 2x = 2 X 3 = 6 cm

V = p X l X t = 15 x 9 x 6 = 810 cm3
