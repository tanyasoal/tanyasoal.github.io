Jawaban

Dik:
s = 1:1.750.000
jp = 6 cm
v = 25 km/jam
tiba = 11.45
istirahat = 30 menit

Dit: berangkat?

Jawab:
s = jp/js
1/1.750.000 = 6/js
1 × js = 6 × 1.750.000
js = 10.500.000cm/100.000
js = 105 km

v = s/t
25 = 105/t
t = 105/25
t = 21/5 jam
t = 4 jam 12 menit

t = tiba - berangkat
4 jam 12 menit = (11.45 + 30menit) + berangkat
4 jam 12 menit = 12.15 + berangkat
berangkat = 08.03

Misalah akar-akarnya a dan b
maka a^3 b^2 + b^3 a^2 = ....?

a^3 b^2 + b^3 a^2
= (a^2 b^2) (a + b)
= (ab)^2 (a+b)
= (c/a)^2 (-b/a)
= ( 1/(-2) )^2 · (-1/(-2))
= 1/4 · 1/2
= 1/8
