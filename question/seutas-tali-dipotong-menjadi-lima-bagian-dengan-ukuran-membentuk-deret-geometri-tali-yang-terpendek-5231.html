Jawaban

Panjang (p), lebar(l), tinggi (t)
pl = 500. p = 500/l

lt = 320. t = 320/l

pt = 400
500/l x 320/l = 400
160000 = 400 l^2
l = √400 = 20

p = 500/20 = 25

t = 320/20= 16

volume= plt = 25 x 20 x 16
