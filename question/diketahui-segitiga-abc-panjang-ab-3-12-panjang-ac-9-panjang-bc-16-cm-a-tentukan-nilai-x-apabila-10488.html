Jawaban

2x^2 - 3x + 5 = 0
a = 2, b = -3, c = 5
X1 × X2 = c/a = 5/2
X1 + X2 = -b/a = 3/2
• (X1 - 3)(X2 - 3) = X1.X2 - 3X1 - 3X2 + 9
= X1.X2 - 3(X1 + X2) + 9
= 5/2 - 3(3/2) + 9
= 5/2 - 9/2 + 9
= 4/2
= 2
• (X1 - 3) + (X2 - 3) = X1 + X2 - 6
= 3/2 - 6
= -9/2
