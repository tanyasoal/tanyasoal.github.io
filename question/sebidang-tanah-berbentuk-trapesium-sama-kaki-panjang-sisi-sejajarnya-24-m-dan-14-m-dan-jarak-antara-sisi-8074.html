Jawaban

V = luas alas × tinggi prisma
= 1/2 × d1 × d2 × t
= 1/2 × 10 × 24 × 20
= 120 ×20
= 2400 cm3


Lp = ( 2 × luas alas ) + ( keliling alas × tinggi )
= ( 2 × 1/2 × d1 × d2 ) + ( 4 × s × t )
= ( 2 × 1/2 × 10 × 24 ) + ( 4 × 13 × 20 )
= 240 + 1040
= 1280 cm2
