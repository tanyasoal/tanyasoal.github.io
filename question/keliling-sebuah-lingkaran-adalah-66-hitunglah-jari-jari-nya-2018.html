v = (3x^2 - 1)^3 --> v' = 3(3x^2 - 1)^2.(6x) = 18x(3x^2 - 1)^2
f'(x) = u'v + v'u = 1(3x^2 - 1)^3 + 18x(3x^2 - 1)^2(x + 1)
f'(0) = (0 - 1)^3 + 0 = -1

A=mata dadu bilangan prima
A={2,3,5}
n(A)=3

=3/6
=1/6
Frekuensi harapan= P×banyak percobaan
=1/6×36
= 6 kali
