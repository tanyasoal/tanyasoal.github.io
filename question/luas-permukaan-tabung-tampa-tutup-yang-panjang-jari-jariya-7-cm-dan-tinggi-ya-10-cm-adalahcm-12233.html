Misal : p = 6a, l = 5a, dan t = 3a
Sehingga :
4(p + l + t) = 168
4(6a + 5a + 3a) = 168
4(14a) = 168
56a = 168
a = 168/56
a = 3

Jadi :
panjang = 6(3) = 18 cm
lebar = 5(3) = 15 cm
tinggi = 3(3) = 9 cm
