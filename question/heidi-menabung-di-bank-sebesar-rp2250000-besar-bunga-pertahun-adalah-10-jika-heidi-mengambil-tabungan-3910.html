Jawaban

Japus^2=GSPD^2+(R+r)^2
24^2=26^2+(4+r)^2
576=676+(4+r)^2
(4+r)^2=100
(4+r)=10
r=10-6
r=4

Jawaban

s = k * n
k = s/n
k = 264000 ÷ 1500 = 176 cm

k = 2 * 22/7 * r
176 * 7/22 = r
28 cm = r
