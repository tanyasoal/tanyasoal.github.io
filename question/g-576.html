Jawaban

(4a^2b^5)^3/(6a^4b^3)^2
= (4^3.(a^2)^3.(b^5)^3)/(6^2.(a^4)^2.(b^3)^2)
= (64.a^6.b^15)/(36.a^8.b^6)
= 16/9.a^(6 - 8).b^(15 - 6)
=16/9.a^-2.b^9
= (16.b^9)/a^2
