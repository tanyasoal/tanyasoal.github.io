Berat rata² 10 org putri = 49 kg

Penyelesaian :
Berat Rata Rata Seluruh Siswa
= ((15 x 53 kg) + (10 x 49 kg)) / (15+10)
= (795 kg + 490 kg) / 25
= 1285 kg / 25
= 51,4 kg

keliling = 2 (p+L)

            = 2 (3L + 1)
            = 6L + 2
6L = 32 - 2
     = 30
L = 30 : 6
   = 5

P = 2L + 1
    = 2 . 5 + 1
    = 10 + 1
    = 11

Luas

= 11 x 5
