Diketahui f(x)= 2x² - 4px+p.jika (x - 1)=12,maka nilai adalah

Jawaban

F(x) = 2x² - 4px + p
f(x-1) = 2(x-1)² - 4p(x-1) + p = 12
= 2(x²-2x+1) - 4px-p + p = 12
= 2x² - 4x + 2 - 4px - p + p = 12
= 2x² - 4x - 4px + 2 = 12
= 2x² - 4x - 4px = 10

Jika x1 & x2 adalah akar akar dari persamaan 2x2-3x+5=0 maka persamaan kuadrat baru yg akar akarnya x1-3 dan x2-3 adalah

Jawaban

2x^2 - 3x + 5 = 0
a = 2, b = -3, c = 5
X1 × X2 = c/a = 5/2
X1 + X2 = -b/a = 3/2
• (X1 - 3)(X2 - 3) = X1.X2 - 3X1 - 3X2 + 9
= X1.X2 - 3(X1 + X2) + 9
= 5/2 - 3(3/2) + 9
= 5/2 - 9/2 + 9
= 4/2
= 2
• (X1 - 3) + (X2 - 3) = X1 + X2 - 6
= 3/2 - 6
= -9/2
