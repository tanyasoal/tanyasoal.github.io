a = 8 cm

Lebar = 3a = 3(8) = 24 cm

l = 3 bagian = 3×8 = 24 cm

Jawaban

Materi : Balok dan Kubus

= 4 (p + l + t)
= 4 (11 + 7 + 9)
= 4 (27)
= 108 cm

= 2 (pl + pt + lt)
