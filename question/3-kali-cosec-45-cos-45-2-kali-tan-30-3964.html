3 kali (cosec 45°)² + (cos 45°)² - 2 kali (tan 30°)²

Jawaban

Kelas 10 Matematika
Bab Trigonometri

3 . (cosec 45°)² + (cos 45°)² - 2 . (tan 30°)²
= 3 . (√2)² + (√2)² - 2 . (1/√3)²
= 3 . 2 + 2 - 2 . 1/3
= 6 + 2 - 2/3
= 8 - 2/3
= 24/3 - 2/3
= 22/3
= 7 1/3

Jawaban

X(mean) = 16
untuk x(mean) berlaku
P.x(mean) - Q = 20
P.16 -Q = 20
16P - Q =20

J(jangkauan) = 6

J = xmaks - xmin = 6
J' = P . Xmaks - Q - (P . Xmin -Q) = 9
J' = P . Xmaks - Q - P . Xmin + Q = 9
<=> P . Xmaks  - P . Xmin = 9
<=> P (Xmaks - Xmin) = 9
<=> P (6) = 9
<=> P = 9/6
P = 3/2

16P-Q = 20
16(3/2) - Q = 20
24 - Q = 20
Q = 24 - 20
Q = 4

2P + Q = 2(3/2) - 4 = 3 - 4 = -1
