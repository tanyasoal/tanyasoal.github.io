Jawaban

R = 6 cm
r = 3 cm
l = 12 cm

l² = p² - (R - r)²
12² = p² - (6 - 3)²
12² = p² - 3²
144 = p² - 9
p² = 144 + 9
p² = 153
p = √153
p = √(9 x 17)
p = 3√17 cm

keliling = 2 (p+L)

            = 2 (3L + 1)

6L = 32 - 2
     = 30
L = 30 : 6
   = 5

P = 2L + 1
