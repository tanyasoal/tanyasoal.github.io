b. 18 cm
c. 16 cm
d. 12 cm

Jawaban

Dik:
L=16 cm
j=20 cm
R=4 cm

r=_______?
penyelesaian:
(R-r)²=j²-L²
(4-r)²=20²-16²
(4-r)²=400-256
4-r=√144
r=4+12
r=16 cm

=>C.16 cm
