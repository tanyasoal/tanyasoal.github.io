wa(air)= 250 N
pair= 1000 kg/m^3

ditanya: Fa & pb?
jawab:
Fa= wa-wu
Fa= 500 - 250
Fa= 250N <= gaya apung

=> cari vol.air/va?
Fa= pair.g.va

=> penyelesaian:
pb= m/va
