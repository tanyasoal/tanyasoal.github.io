Jawaban

Luas jajargenjang = a x t
= 15 cm x 18 cm = 270 cm persegi
volume = a x t (jajar genjang ) x t ( prisma )
= 15 x 18 x 20
=270 x 20
=5400 cm kubik
Pertama cari rumus jajargenjang
rumus:l=a×t
l=15cm×18cm
=250 cm

=250×20
=5000 cm
