Jawaban

Lp balok = 2×(p×l+p×t+l×t)
550 = 2×(15×10+15×t+10×t)
550 = 2×(150+15t+10t)
550 = 2×(150+25t)
550 = 300+50t
550-300 = 50t
250 = 50t
t = 250/50 = 50 cm
Lp = 2(pl+pt+lt)
550 = 2(15.10+15t+10t)
550 = 2(150+25t)
550 = 300+50t
550-300 = 50t
250 = 50t
250 : 50 = t
5 = t
