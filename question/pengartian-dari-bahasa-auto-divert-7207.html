A: What's wrong? 
H: I have a problem deciding which is suitable.
A: OK, what do you have in mind? 
H: I think my mom likes the string collection, but I also have another idea, buying her an oven. 
A: Why don't you buy her both? 
H: That's beyond my budget. 
A: Hmm, I'd say an oven would be great. 
H: You think so? Yet, it's too big. 
A: So, get the string collection then. 
H: You're not helping. I'm more confused now. 
A: Well, I've just given you idea.
