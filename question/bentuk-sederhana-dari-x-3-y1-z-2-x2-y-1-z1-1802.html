Diketahui f(x)= 2x² - 4px+p.jika (x - 1)=12,maka nilai adalah

Jawaban

F(x) = 2x² - 4px + p
f(x-1) = 2(x-1)² - 4p(x-1) + p = 12
= 2(x²-2x+1) - 4px-p + p = 12
= 2x² - 4x + 2 - 4px - p + p = 12
= 2x² - 4x - 4px + 2 = 12
= 2x² - 4x - 4px = 10
