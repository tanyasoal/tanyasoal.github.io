A.cos 70°
B.sin 70°

Jawaban

Diketahui
cos 35 = p/q
sin 35 = (√(q^2 - p^2))/q ---> pakai aturan phytagotas
A.
cos 2A = (cos A) ^2 - (sin A) ^2 ---> rumusan penyelesaian
cos 70 = cos 2.(35)
= (cos 35)^2 - (sin 35)^2
= (p/q)^2 - ((√(q^2 - p^2))/q)^2
= p^2/q^2 - (q^2 - p^2)/q^2
= (p^2 - (q^2 - p^2))/q^2
= (p^2 - q^2 + p^2))/q^2
= (2p^2 - q^2)/q^2
