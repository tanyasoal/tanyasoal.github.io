9. Gunakan rumus basa lemah

pH = 12 ----> pOH = 2 ---> [OH-] = 0.01 M

pH = 11 ----> [OH-] = 0.001 M

[OH-] stlh pencampuran:
(100×0.01 + 50×0.001)/150 = 0.007 M

pOH = -log 0.007 = 3 - log 7

pH campuran = 11 + log 7

10. pH = 2 ---> [H+] = 0.01 M
pH = 3 ----> [H+] = 0.001 M
Terjadi pengenceran 10x shg volume total akhir = 10x vol. awal
Jadi penambahan air sebesar 900 mL
