What is Sinai Covenant?

Jawaban

The Mosaic Covenant is a conditional covenant made between God and the nation of Israel at Mount Sinai (Exodus 19-24). It is sometimes called the Sinai Covenant but is more often referred to as the Mosaic Covenant since Moses was God’s chosen leader of Israel at that time. The pattern of the covenant is very similar to other ancient covenants of that time because it is between a sovereign king (God) and his people or subjects (Israel). At the time of the covenant, God reminded the people of their obligation to be obedient to His law (Exodus 19:5), and the people agreed to the covenant when they said, “All that the Lord has spoken we will do!” (Exodus 19:8). This covenant would serve to set the nation of Israel apart from all other nations as God’s chosen people and was as equally binding as the unconditional covenant that God made with Abraham because it is also a blood covenant. The Mosaic Covenant is a significant covenant in both God’s redemptive history and in the history of the nation of Israel through whom God would sovereignly choose to bless the world with both His written Word and the Living Word, Jesus Christ.

Berikut adalah paragraf tersebut dengan kata-kata dalam titik-titik yang sudah dilengkapi: I have a special friend. She is my classmate and sits beside me. Her name is Ratih. She is a beautiful girl and very simple to look at. However, I really admire her. She is not only kind but also tough.Ratih comes from a very poor family. Her father is a porter and her mother has passed away. She has a little brother. His name is Ranu. In order to help their father, Ratih and her brother work hard to earn some money. Ratih sells food during our class break while her brother sells newspapers and magazines after school.One thing that I always admire about her is that she can manage her time well and she always is cheerful.PembahasanUntuk dapat mengisi titik-titik dengan kata-kata yang tepat , kita terlebih dahulu harus memahami keseluruhan isi teks bacaan tersebut beserta konteksnya. Karena teks tersebut terdapat dalam bahasa Inggris, maka pembaca harus mengerti bahasa Inggris yang digunakan, dan apabila perlu menerjemahkannya secara sederhana ke dalam bahasa Indonesia untuk dapat memahami isinya.Kata-kata yang tepat untuk digunakan untuk mengisi titik-titik adalah kata-kata yang sesuai dengan konteks kalimat terkait. Misalnya:"One thing that I always admire about her is that she can ... her time well..."kalimat tersebut berarti "Satu hal yang selalu aku kagumi tentang dia adalah bahwa dia dapat ... waktunya dengan baik..."Ketika memahami kalimat tersebut, kita dapat mengetahui bahwa kosa kata yang tepat untuk mengisi bagian yang kosong adalah "mengatur", dalam bahasa Inggrisnya "manage".Pelajari lebih lanjut
