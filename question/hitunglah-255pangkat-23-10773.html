Hitunglah (25√5)pangkat -2/3?

Jawaban

(25√5)^(-2/3)
= ((5^2).(5^(1/2)))^(-2/3)
= (5^(2 + 1/2))^(-2/3)
= (5^(5/2))^(-2/3)
= 5^((5/2).(-2/3))
= 5^(-5/3)
= 1/(5^(5/3))
= 1/(5^(1 + 2/3))
= 1/(5^1.5^(2/3))
= 1/(5.25^(1/3))
