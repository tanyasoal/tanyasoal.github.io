Keliling persegi panjang
k = 2 ( p + l )
44 = 2 ( 3x - 2 + x + 4 )
44 = 2 ( 4x + 2 )
44 = 8x + 4
44 - 4 = 8x
40 = 8x
x = 40 / 8
x = 5

Ukuran panjang
= 3x - 2
= 3(5) - 2
= 15 - 2
= 13 meter

Ukuran lebar
= x + 4
= 5 + 4
= 9 meter
