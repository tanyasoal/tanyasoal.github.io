B. 1,25 m/s2
C. 1,50 m/s2
D. 1,66 m/s2

Jawaban

Diket
Ma = 500 kg
Aa = 2m/s2
Fa = Ma. Aa
= 500×2
=1000 newton
Mb= 300 Kg + 500 kg = 800 kg
Fb=Fa= 1000 newton
Ab = Fb/Mb
=1000/800
=1,25 m/s2
