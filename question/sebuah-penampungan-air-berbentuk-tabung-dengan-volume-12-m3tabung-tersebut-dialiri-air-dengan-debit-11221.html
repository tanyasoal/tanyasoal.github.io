Luas

= 400 m2

Panjang sisi

= √400 m2

= 20 m

Panjang pada gambar

= 20 m × 1/250

= 2.000 cm : 250

= 8 cm

Keliling

= 4 × s

= 4 × 8 cm

= 32 cm

waktu = 1/4 jam

          = 3 x 4
          = 12 m³/jam
Volume (v) = 3 m³
waktu (t) = 1/4 jam
debit = .....?
debit = v/t
debit = 3 m³ / ¼ jam
