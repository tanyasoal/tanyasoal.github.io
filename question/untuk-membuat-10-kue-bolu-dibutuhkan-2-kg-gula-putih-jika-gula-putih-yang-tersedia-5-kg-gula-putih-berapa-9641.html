the area of a rectangle is equal to the area of a square whose sides have length 8 cm. if the width of the rectangle = 4cm , then find the length of the rectangle

Jawaban

Square = s.s
rectangle = w.l

s.s = w.l
8.8 = w.4
64 = w.4
64/4 = w
w = 16 cm
