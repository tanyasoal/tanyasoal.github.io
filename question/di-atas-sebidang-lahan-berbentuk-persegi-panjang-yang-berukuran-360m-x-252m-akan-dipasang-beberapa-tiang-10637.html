Jawaban

Materi = Bangun Ruang

12 . Rusuk = Jumlah panjang rusuk
12 . Rusuk = 96 cm
Rusuk = 96 cm / 12
Rusuk = 8 cm

Luas Permukaan Kubus
= 6(S x S)
= 6(8 cm x 8 cm)
= 6(64 cm²)
= 384 cm²

Volume Kubus
= S x S x S
= 8 cm x 8 cm x 8 cm
= 512 cm²
