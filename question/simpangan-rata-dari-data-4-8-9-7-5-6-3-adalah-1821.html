Panjang sebenarnya

= 1 : 1/200

= 1 × 200

= 200 cm

= 2 m

Lebar sebenarnya

= 4,5 : 1/200

= 4,5 × 200

= 900 cm

= 9 m

Luas sebenarnya

= 2 m × 9 m

= 18 m2
Panjang sebenarnya =

= P. p : s

= 1 : 1/200

= 1 × 200/1

= 200 cm

= 2 m

Lebar sebenarnya =

= L. p : s

= 45/10 : 1/200

= 45/10 × 200/1

= 9000/10 (900cm)

= 9 m

Luas = p × l

Luas = 2 × 9

Luas = 18 m² ✔
