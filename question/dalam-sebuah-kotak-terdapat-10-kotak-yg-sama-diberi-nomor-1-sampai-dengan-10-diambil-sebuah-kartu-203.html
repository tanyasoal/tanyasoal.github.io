a panjang rusuk kubus
b panjang kerangka kubus
c volume kubus

Jawaban

Kelas 6 Matematika
Bab Kubus dan Balok

a] Luas permukaan = 216
6 x s x s = 216
s² = 216/6
s = √36
s = 6 cm

b] Panjang kerangka kubus
= 12 x s
= 12 x 6
= 72 cm

c] Volume
= s x s x s
= 6 x 6 x 6
= 216 cm^3

= √36 cm2

= 6 cm

= 6 cm × 12 rusuk

c ) Volume kubus

= s × s × s

= 6 cm × 6 cm × 6 cm

= 216 cm3
