Tentukan g(x) jika diketahui (fog)(x)=4x^6-12x^3+6 dan f(x)=x^2-4x+1

Jawaban

(fog)(x) = 4x^6 - 12x³ + 6

(f(x) - 2)² - 3 = 4x^6 - 12x³ + 6
(f(x) - 2)² = 4x^6 - 12x³ + 6 + 3
(f(x) - 2)² = 4x^6 - 12x³ + 9
