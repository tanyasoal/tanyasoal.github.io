◼ Nilai Suku pertama

U5 = 7
a + 4b = 7
a + 4(13/4) = 7
a + 13 = 7
a = 7 - 13
a = -5

Un = a + (n - 1)b

U17 = -5 + (17 - 1)(13/4)
U17 = -5 + 16(13/4)

U17 = -5 + 52
