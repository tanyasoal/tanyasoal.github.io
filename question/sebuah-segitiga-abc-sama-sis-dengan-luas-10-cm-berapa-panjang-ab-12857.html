Jawaban

Luas

= s × s

= 400 m2

Panjang sisi

= √400 m2

= 20 m

Panjang pada gambar

= 20 m × 1/250

= 2.000 cm : 250

= 8 cm

Keliling

= 4 × s

= 4 × 8 cm

= 32 cm
