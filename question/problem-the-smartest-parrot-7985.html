Pertanyaan Terkait

Direction: identification all the subject, verb, direct object, indirect object,complement, and modifier from the sentenses below. 1. The girl does not feel good.
2. The waitress served spaghetti and latte for them.
3. The bride threw the bouquet of flower to the bridesmaids.
4. The store opened early yesterday.
5. The math book lays on the professor's desk.

Jawaban

S = subject
V = verb
DO = direct object
IO = indirect object
C = complement
M = modifier

1
S = the girl
V = feel
C = good

2.
S = the waitress
V = served
DO = spaghetti and latte
IO = for them

3.
S = The bride
V = threw
DO = the bouquet of flowers
IO = to the bridesmaids

4.
S = the store
V = opened
M = early
M = yesterday

5
S = the math book
V = lies
M = on the professor's desk
