Jawaban

9x² + 25y² + 18x - 100y = 116
9x² + 18x + 25y² - 100y = 116
9(x² + 2x) + 25(y² - 4y) = 116
9(x² + 2x + 1) + 25(y² - 4y + 4) = 116 + 9(1) + 25(4)
9(x + 1)² + 25(y - 2)² = 225
