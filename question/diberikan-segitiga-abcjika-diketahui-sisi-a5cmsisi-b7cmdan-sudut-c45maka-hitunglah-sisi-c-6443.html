Jawaban

X^2 + y^2 -4x -8y + 15 = 0
(x - 2)^2 - 2^2 + (y -4)^2 - 4^2 + 15 = 0
(x - 2)^2 + (y - 4)^2 - 4 - 16 + 15 = 0
