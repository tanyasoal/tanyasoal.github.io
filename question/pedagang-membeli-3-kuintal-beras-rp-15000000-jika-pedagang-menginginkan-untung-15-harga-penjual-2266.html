Jawaban

60 : 60+20 = 1/12 : 1/x
60 : 80 = 1/12 : 1/x
60 × 1/x = 80 × 1/12
60/x = 80/12
60 × 12 = 80 × x
720 = 80x
720/80 = x
x = 9

Jadi persediaan makanan akan habis dalam 9 hari

60×1÷× = 80 × 1 ÷ 12
60÷× =80 ÷ 12
60 × 12 = 80× x
720 =80×
720÷80=x
x=9
