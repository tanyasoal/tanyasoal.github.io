Jawaban

1.
(+) He is a very smart boy
(-) He isn't a smart boy
(?) Is he a smart boy?

2.
(+) They do their homework at the library together
(-) They don't do their homework at the library together
(?) Do they do their homework at the library together?

3.
(+) I bring my lunch to school today
(-) I don't bring my lunch to school today
(?) Do I bring my lunch today?

4.
(+) She borrows my dictionary
(-) She doesn't borrow my dictionary
(?) Does she borrow my dictionary?

5.
(+) My uncle asks me to go fishing
(-) My uncle doesn't ask me to go fishing
(?) Does my uncle ask me to go fishing?
