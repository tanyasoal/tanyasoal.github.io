Jawaban

Diagonal Sisi = n ( n - 3 ) ÷ 2
35 = n ( n - 3 ) ÷ 2
35 = 10 ( 10 - 3 ) ÷ 2
= ( 10 x 7 ) ÷ 2
= 70 ÷ 2
= 35

n = 10

Banyak Rusuk
= 2 x n
= 2 x 10
= 20
